ABOUT


We grew up in Southern China, where the cuisine was influenced by our bustling culture and experiences. This is a mom and pop restaurant located in the heart of Chinatown. We make traditional Chinese and Vietnamese food from scratch so please be patient with us when we are busy. We use the freshest ingredients sourced from the markets of Chinatown so please join us for a traditional experience served in a modern dining setting.
